Common Problems

How to Login

If you're having trouble logging in, make sure you are using the correct username and password. If you forgot your credentials, use the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page.

Where to Check Your License

To check your license status, log in to your account and navigate to the 'My Licenses' section from the dashboard.

Calculating Your Total

To calculate your total, go to the 'Billing' section where you can see a detailed breakdown of your charges and total amount.
